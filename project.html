Our project

Our project is to use advertisement as a way to make money. We will use this money to fund some charities. It will also be invested to increase the available funding for the associations. We will specially fund organisations fighting for the environment.

How ?

We are using advertisements to generate money just like Youtube or Utip do. Thanks to this system you can give money to charities for free just like you are supporting some Youtuber or Artist simply by watching ads. We manage to place all advertisement in only one page so you can enjoy a clean website.
We will use this money by giving it directly to charities, investing it to create even more fund in the end and that's all for the moment - no money goes into our pocket because it's for a good cause 👍
