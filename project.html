Our project

Our project is to use advertisement as a way to make money. We will use this money to fund some charities. It will also be invested to increase the available funding for the associations. We will specially fund organisations fighting for the environment.

How ?

We use the ad-system built in Brave so you just have to change your navigator to start making a donation. To start generating money you have to download Brave and enable ads, they you can use it to make some donation directly to Peeco (btw you can make this monthly 😉). We then collect this money and invest it into differents cryptos or stocks. We are only investing in things we think are safe.

Why ?

Why us ? That's a good question and a very simple answer we are just different from the other charities because we aren't one and we aren't reliated to any charities. So we are basically an independant organisations that can support more than one charities with many different goals and objective such as ecology or humanitarian aid. Further more we are helping association in the long term because we are investing our money. It something very simple but you make money with money so more we have money more we can make money and get return on investement. All of our investement will be some low risk investement to reduce the risk of a bad surprise and the waste of money.
